About Brew & Bean

We started as a tiny corner shop with one espresso machine and a dream. Today, we source beans directly from small farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — roasting them in-house every week.

Whether you're grabbing a quick latte or settling in with a book, Brew & Bean is your neighborhood coffee home.
